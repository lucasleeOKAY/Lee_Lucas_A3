Splatoon is my most played series, having the most hours in Splatoon 2 out of the three volumes. I started playing in 2018, quickly falling in love with the game and decided that I had to take it a little more seriously. I joined a team, played tournaments, and met amazing people.

The stories, the rivalries, the history, there is so much that goes into this silly game about painting that I appreciate and I am so grateful it allowed me to develop so many strong connections with people I can consider long term friends.
